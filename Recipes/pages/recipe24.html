Crispy Beef Bites with Fries
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs sirloin steak, cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 4 large potatoes, cut into fries
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ketchup for serving
  • Green onions for garnish

Procedures:

  1. Season beef cubes with salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder
  2. Set up breading station: flour, eggs, breadcrumbs
  3. Coat each beef cube in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs
  4. Heat oil to 350Â°F
  5. Fry beef bites in batches for 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy
  6. Drain on paper towels
  7. Fry potatoes in same oil until golden (5-6 minutes)
  8. Season fries with salt immediately after frying
  9. Arrange beef bites and fries on plate
  10. Garnish with sliced green onions, serve with ketchup
