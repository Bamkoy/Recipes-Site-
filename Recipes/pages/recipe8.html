Mango Smoothie Parfait
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • Mango syrup or caramel sauce for drizzling
  • Extra mango chunks for topping

Procedures:

  1. Reserve some mango chunks for topping
  2. Blend remaining mangoes, yogurt, milk, honey, and ice until smooth
  3. Pour smoothie into tall cups, filling halfway
  4. Add a layer of whipped cream
  5. Add more mango chunks
  6. Fill cup with remaining smoothie
  7. Top with generous amount of whipped cream
  8. Pile mango chunks on top
  9. Drizzle with mango syrup or caramel
  10. Insert straws and serve immediately
