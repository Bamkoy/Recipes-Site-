Bacon Double Cheeseburger
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 4 brioche buns
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Pickles

Special Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tsp paprika

Procedures:

  1. Form beef into 8 thin patties, season with salt and pepper
  2. Cook bacon until crispy, set aside
  3. Mix all sauce ingredients together
  4. Heat griddle or large pan over medium-high heat
  5. Cook patties 3-4 minutes per side
  6. Add cheese slices in last minute to melt
  7. Toast buns face-down on griddle
  8. Spread sauce on both bun halves
  9. Layer: bottom bun, lettuce, tomato, two patties, bacon, onion, pickles, top bun
  10. Secure with toothpick, serve immediately
