Lamb Chops (or Rack of Lamb)
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 8 lamb rib chops
  • 2 lbs Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • Salt and pepper

Procedures:

  1. Season lamb chops with salt, pepper, and rosemary
  2. Boil potatoes until tender, drain
  3. Mash potatoes with cream, 2 tbsp butter, salt, and pepper
  4. Blanch asparagus in boiling water for 3 minutes
  5. Heat pan over high heat with olive oil
  6. Sear lamb chops 3-4 minutes per side for medium
  7. Remove lamb, add wine to deglaze pan
  8. Add stock, reduce by half to make sauce
  9. Saut√© asparagus in remaining butter with garlic
  10. Plate mashed potatoes, top with lamb and asparagus, drizzle with sauce
