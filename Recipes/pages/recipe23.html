Korean Beef Rice Bowl
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ribeye or sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 carrots, julienned and glazed
  • 2 cups baby potatoes, roasted
  • 2 cups fresh greens
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds

Procedures:

  1. Mix soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, and garlic for marinade
  2. Marinate beef slices for 20 minutes
  3. Roast potatoes with olive oil at 400°F for 25 minutes
  4. Glaze carrots by sautéing with butter, honey, and soy sauce
  5. Heat pan over high heat, cook beef 2-3 minutes until caramelized
  6. Fry eggs sunny-side up
  7. Divide rice among bowls
  8. Arrange beef, potatoes, carrots, and greens around rice
  9. Top with fried egg
  10. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds
