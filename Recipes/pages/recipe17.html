Tomahawk Steak BBQ Platter
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 tomahawk ribeye steak (2-3 lbs)
  • 1 lb jumbo shrimp
  • 4 ears corn
  • 1 lb baby potatoes
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, thyme)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Hot sauce for serving

Procedures:

  1. Season tomahawk steak with salt and pepper, let rest 30 minutes
  2. Preheat grill to high heat
  3. Grill steak 6-7 minutes per side for medium-rare
  4. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing
  5. Boil potatoes until tender, then grill for char
  6. Grill corn, turning to char all sides
  7. Melt butter with garlic and herbs
  8. Grill shrimp 2-3 minutes per side, brush with garlic butter
  9. Slice steak against the grain
  10. Arrange all components on large platter, serve with hot sauce
