Spicy Beef Noodles
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, sliced into strips
  • 12 oz egg noodles
  • 2 bell peppers (red and yellow), julienned
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili oil
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Procedures:

  1. Marinate beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce and chili oil for 20 minutes
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions, drain
  3. Mix remaining soy sauce, Sriracha, brown sugar, and rice vinegar for sauce
  4. Heat wok over high heat with vegetable oil
  5. Stir-fry beef strips for 3-4 minutes until caramelized
  6. Remove beef, set aside
  7. Add peppers, garlic, and ginger, stir-fry 2 minutes
  8. Return beef to wok, add noodles and sauce
  9. Toss everything for 2 minutes until well coated
  10. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds, serve immediately
