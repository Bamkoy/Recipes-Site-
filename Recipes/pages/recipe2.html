Lobster Recipe
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 live lobsters (1 to 1.5 pounds each)
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1/4 cup sea salt
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
Procedure:
  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add sea salt to the water.
  2. Grasp the lobsters firmly and quickly plunge them headfirst into the boiling water.
  3. Cover the pot and return the water to a boil. Cook the lobsters for about 8-10 minutes for the first pound and 3 additional minutes for each additional pound.
  4. Remove the lobsters from the pot using tongs and let them drain in a colander.
  5. Serve the lobsters hot with melted butter, lemon wedges, and a sprinkle of fresh parsley if desired.
