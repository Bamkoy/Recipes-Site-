Beef Burrito
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1 cup cheese, shredded
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Procedures:

  1. Cook rice according to package directions
  2. Brown ground beef in large skillet
  3. Add onions, peppers, and garlic, cook until soft
  4. Season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper
  5. Add black beans, heat through
  6. Warm tortillas in dry pan or microwave
  7. Layer rice, beef mixture, and cheese on each tortilla
  8. Fold in sides, then roll tightly from bottom
  9. Optional: grill burrito seam-side down for 2 minutes to crisp
  10. Slice in half, serve with sour cream and salsa
