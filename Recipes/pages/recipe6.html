Pan-Seared Cod with French Fries
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 thick cod fillets (8 oz each)
  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Tartar Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp dill pickles, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp capers, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Procedures:

  1. Cut potatoes into fries, soak in cold water for 30 minutes
  2. Pat fish dry, season with salt, pepper, and paprika
  3. Heat oil to 350Â°F, fry potatoes in batches until golden (4-5 minutes)
  4. Drain on paper towels and season with salt
  5. Heat butter in pan over medium-high heat
  6. Add garlic, cook until fragrant
  7. Place cod in pan, cook 4-5 minutes per side until golden and flaky
  8. Baste with garlic butter while cooking
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley
  10. Mix all tartar sauce ingredients, serve alongside fish and fries
