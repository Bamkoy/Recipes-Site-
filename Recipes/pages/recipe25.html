Beef and Broccoli Noodles
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 12 oz noodles (lo mein or udon)
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Procedures:

  1. Mix beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce and cornstarch, marinate 15 minutes
  2. Cook noodles according to package, drain and set aside
  3. Blanch broccoli in boiling water for 2 minutes, drain
  4. Heat wok over high heat with vegetable oil
  5. Stir-fry beef until browned, remove and set aside
  6. Add more oil, stir-fry broccoli and peppers for 2 minutes
  7. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds
  8. Return beef to wok, add noodles
  9. Pour in remaining soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin, and sesame oil
  10. Toss for 2 minutes, garnish with sesame seeds
