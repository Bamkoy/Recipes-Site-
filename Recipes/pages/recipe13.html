Beef And Vegetable kebabs
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 bell peppers (red, yellow, orange), cut into chunks
  • 2 onions, cut into chunks
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water)

Procedures:

  1. Mix ground beef, egg, breadcrumbs, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper
  2. Form into meatballs (about 2 inches diameter)
  3. Thread meatballs onto skewers alternating with peppers and onions
  4. Brush vegetables with olive oil
  5. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  6. Grill kebabs for 12-15 minutes, turning every 3-4 minutes
  7. Ensure meatballs reach internal temperature of 160Â°F
  8. Remove from grill, let rest 2 minutes
  9. Serve hot with rice or flatbread
