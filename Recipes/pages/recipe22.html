Stuffed Pancakes (Meat-Filled)
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Filling:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Procedures:

  1. Whisk flour, eggs, milk, butter, and salt until smooth batter forms
  2. Let batter rest for 15 minutes
  3. Brown ground beef with onions, peppers, and garlic
  4. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper
  5. Heat non-stick pan over medium heat, lightly grease
  6. Pour 1/4 cup batter, swirl to coat pan
  7. Cook until edges lift (1-2 minutes), flip and cook 30 seconds more
  8. Repeat with remaining batter
  9. Place 2-3 tbsp filling on each pancake, roll tightly
  10. Serve warm with dipping sauce on the side
