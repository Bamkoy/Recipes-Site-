Ultimate BBQ Platter
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks baby back ribs
  • BBQ rub (paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder)
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce

Sides:

  • 4 corn on the cob
  • 8 hot dogs
  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 2 lbs French fries
  • 8 tortillas
  • Lettuce, tomatoes, cheese for wraps

Procedures:

  1. Rub ribs with spice mix, wrap in foil, bake at 275°F for 2.5 hours
  2. Unwrap, brush with BBQ sauce, broil until caramelized
  3. Boil corn for 5 minutes, then grill for char marks
  4. Season wings, bake at 400°F for 40 minutes, toss in BBQ sauce
  5. Grill hot dogs until charred
  6. Deep fry potatoes at 350°F until golden
  7. Prepare wraps with grilled chicken, lettuce, tomato, and cheese
  8. Arrange everything on large platter
  9. Serve with extra BBQ sauce and condiments
