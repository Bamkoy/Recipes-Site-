Nigerian Pepper Soup With Plaintains
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs goat meat or beef, cubed
  • 2 ripe plantains, peeled and halved
  • 2 yams, peeled and cubed
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 3 tbsp pepper soup spice mix
  • 2 stock cubes
  • 6 cups water
  • Fresh basil leaves (scent leaves)
  • Salt to taste

Procedures:

  1. Wash meat thoroughly, place in large pot
  2. Add onions, stock cubes, and 1 tbsp pepper soup spice
  3. Cover with water, bring to boil
  4. Reduce heat, simmer for 45 minutes until meat is tender
  5. Add yam cubes, cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add remaining pepper soup spice and scotch bonnets
  7. Add plantains, cook for 8-10 minutes
  8. Adjust seasoning with salt
  9. Add fresh basil leaves
  10. Serve hot in bowls with extra peppers on the side
