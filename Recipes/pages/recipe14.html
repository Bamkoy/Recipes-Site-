BBQ Chicken Pizza
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Toppings:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup smoked gouda (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt
  2. Add water and oil, knead for 8 minutes until smooth
  3. Let dough rise for 1 hour until doubled
  4. Toss chicken with 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  5. Preheat oven to 475Â°F with pizza stone if available
  6. Roll dough into 12-inch circle
  7. Spread remaining BBQ sauce on dough
  8. Add chicken, onions, and cheese
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving
