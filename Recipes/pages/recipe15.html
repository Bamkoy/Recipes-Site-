Surf And Turf (Steak And Shrimp)
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 filet mignon steaks (8 oz each)
  • 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Procedures:

  1. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper
  2. Heat cast iron skillet over high heat with olive oil
  3. Sear steaks 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare
  4. Remove steaks, let rest
  5. In same pan, melt 2 tbsp butter
  6. Add garlic, cook until fragrant
  7. Add shrimp, cook 2-3 minutes per side
  8. Add remaining butter, lemon juice, and parsley
  9. Create sauce by swirling pan
  10. Plate steak with shrimp on top, drizzle with garlic butter sauce
