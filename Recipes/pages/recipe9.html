Steak And Eggs With Potatoes
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 strip steaks (8 oz each)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 lb baby potatoes, halved
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper, let rest 20 minutes
  2. Boil potatoes until fork-tender (10-12 minutes), drain
  3. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in pan, roast potatoes until golden
  4. Remove potatoes, add 1 tbsp butter to same pan
  5. Heat another pan with 1 tbsp olive oil over high heat
  6. Sear steaks 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare
  7. Add garlic and remaining butter, baste steaks
  8. Remove steaks, let rest 5 minutes
  9. Fry eggs sunny-side up in remaining butter
  10. Plate steaks with eggs on top, potatoes on side, season eggs with pepper
