Salmon Bowl(or Salad Ni√ßoise-style bowl)
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 4 cups fresh arugula
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, halved
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs (dill, parsley)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Heat pan over medium-high heat
  3. Cook salmon skin-side down for 4-5 minutes
  4. Flip and cook another 3-4 minutes until desired doneness
  5. Brush with herb mixture (remaining olive oil, lemon juice, chopped herbs)
  6. Arrange arugula in bowls as base
  7. Top with salmon, eggs, avocado, tomatoes, olives, and cucumber
  8. Drizzle with extra herb dressing
  9. Season with black pepper and serve immediately
