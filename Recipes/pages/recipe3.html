Sharwarma Recipe
Ingredients/Procedures

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into strips
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the chicken strips to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken strips for 5-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  4. Serve the chicken in warm pita bread with your favorite toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and tahini sauce.
