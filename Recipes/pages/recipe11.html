Roasted Chicken With Vegetables
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 lbs potato wedges
  • 2 cups coleslaw mix
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • Lemon wedge
  • Salt and pepper

Procedures:

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F
  2. Mix olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper
  3. Coat chicken thoroughly with spice mixture
  4. Toss potato wedges with olive oil, salt, and herbs
  5. Place chicken and potatoes on baking sheet
  6. Roast for 35-40 minutes until chicken is golden and cooked through
  7. Prepare coleslaw with mayo, vinegar, and sugar
  8. Toss salad greens with tomatoes
  9. Arrange chicken, potatoes, coleslaw, and salad on platter
  10. Garnish with lemon wedge and fresh parsley
