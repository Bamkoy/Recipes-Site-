Healthy Salmon Power Bowl
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 salmon fillet (6 oz)
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 avocado, halved
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon wedge

Procedures:

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F
  2. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and herbs
  3. Toss chickpeas with olive oil, turmeric, garlic powder, salt
  4. Roast chickpeas for 20 minutes until crispy
  5. Steam or roast broccoli until tender but crisp
  6. Pan-sear salmon skin-side down for 5 minutes
  7. Flip and cook 3 more minutes until done
  8. Arrange spinach in bowl
  9. Top with salmon, broccoli, chickpeas, and avocado
  10. Season avocado with salt and pepper, squeeze lemon over everything
