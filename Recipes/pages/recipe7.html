Grilled Octopus with Lemon
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs fresh or frozen octopus
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 lemons (2 for juice, 1 for garnish)
  • 3 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. If using frozen octopus, thaw in refrigerator overnight
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil with bay leaf, onion, and garlic
  3. Add octopus, reduce heat and simmer for 45-60 minutes until tender
  4. Remove octopus, let cool slightly, then cut into individual tentacles
  5. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper in bowl
  6. Toss octopus in marinade, let sit for 15 minutes
  7. Heat grill or grill pan to high heat
  8. Grill octopus for 3-4 minutes per side until charred
  9. Transfer to serving plate, drizzle with extra olive oil and lemon juice
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon wedges
