Tacos Recipe
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa

Procedure:

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
    Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water according to the packet instructions.
    Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling each tortilla with the cooked beef, lettuce,
    tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
