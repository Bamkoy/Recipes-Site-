Beef Stir Fry
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 12 oz lo mein or egg noodles
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 green bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Cook noodles according to package directions, drain and set aside
  2. Marinate beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce, cornstarch, and pepper for 15 minutes
  3. Heat wok or large pan over high heat with 1 tbsp oil
  4. Stir-fry beef until browned (2-3 minutes), remove and set aside
  5. Add remaining oil, stir-fry peppers and onions for 2 minutes
  6. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 30 seconds
  7. Return beef to pan, add noodles
  8. Pour in remaining soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil
  9. Toss everything together for 2 minutes until well combined
  10. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds if desired
