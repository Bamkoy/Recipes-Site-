Beef Cannelloni
Ingredient/ Procedure

Ingredients:

  • 12 cannelloni tubes
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Procedures:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic, season with salt and pepper
  3. Mix ricotta, half the Parmesan, egg, and basil in bowl
  4. Combine meat with cheese mixture
  5. Spread 1 cup marinara in baking dish
  6. Fill cannelloni tubes with meat mixture
  7. Arrange tubes in dish
  8. Pour remaining marinara over top
  9. Sprinkle with mozzarella and remaining Parmesan
  10. Cover with foil, bake 30 minutes, uncover and bake 15 more minutes until bubbly
